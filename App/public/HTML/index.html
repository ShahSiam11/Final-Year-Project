Attendance Meets Innovation



Smart & Contactless Attendance Tracking

Say goodbye to outdated attendance methods! Our AI-powered system uses facial recognition to automate attendance tracking with speed and accuracy. It eliminates the need for manual check-ins while ensuring a seamless experience for students, employees, and administrators.

Advanced Security with Spoof Detection

Security is at the core of our system. With AI-driven spoof detection, it prevents fraudulent check-ins by detecting fake faces, photos, and videos. Ensuring that only authorized individuals are marked present.

Effortless Attendance Management

Our intuitive dashboard simplifies attendance tracking for students, faculty, and admins. Organizations can customize attendance policies, monitor real-time data, and generate detailed reports—all from one seamless platform. Whether for classrooms or corporate offices, managing attendance has never been easier.






Cutting-Edge AI Infrastructure

Powered by advanced AI models and optimized for real-time processing, our system ensures lightning-fast face recognition and attendance marking. With secure cloud integration and edge computing capabilities, organizations can rely on uninterrupted performance, whether in a single classroom or across multiple locations.


No lag, no delays—just seamless and accurate attendance tracking.





Easy to Use

Attendease is designed with simplicity in mind. Seamlessly integrate it with any camera—whether it's a high-end security system or a basic webcam. No complicated setups, no steep learning curves—just plug and play. Faculty and students get a smooth, hassle-free experience every time.

Reliable & Secure

Our system ensures 24/7 reliability with advanced health checks. Admins can monitor system performance, detect potential issues, and ensure smooth operations. With built-in spoof detection, real-time monitoring, Attendease guarantees secure and accurate attendance tracking.

Powerful Insights & Customization

The dashboard provides a clear, summarized analysis of attendance trends, making it easy for faculty to manage classes and students efficiently. Admins have full control—configuring policies, customizing settings, and adding new rules to fit any organization’s needs. With real-time analytics Attendease empowers users to make informed decisions effortlessly.

    100% Open Source, No Hidden Costs

    Attendease is fully open-source, meaning no reliance on external APIs—just pure, transparent AI. With zero licensing fees or subscription costs, you have complete control over customization and deployment. Built with machine learning, our anti-spoofing model ensures accurate and secure attendance tracking while continuously evolving with community contributions.

    Works with Any Camera

    Attendease seamlessly integrates with any camera—wired, IP, or wireless—giving you the flexibility to use existing hardware without extra investment. Whether you're using a high-end security system or a simple webcam, our AI adapts for optimal performance.

    Dynamic & Multi-Device Friendly

    Access Attendease from anywhere! Whether on a phone, tablet, laptop, or desktop, the system automatically adjusts to any screen size. Faculty and admins can manage attendance effortlessly, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices.

    Effortless Deployment & Customization

    Attendease is designed for quick setup, requiring minimal configuration. Admins can define policies, set up attendance rules, and integrate security features with ease.
